Iron Triangle of Project Management
- Tug of war between competing priorities in a Project
- Three main competing priorities
    * Time
    * Cost
    * Quality / Scope
        + We should always prioritize the MVP (Minimum Viable Product) - What makes it usable and functioning for the user?
